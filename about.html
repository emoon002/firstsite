About Me

Thank you for visiting my website! My name is Evan Moon and I am a student web developer located in Las Vegas, Nevada. I am originally from Thousand Oaks, California, but moved to Las Vegas while I finish school and decide where to dig in and pursue my career as a web developer. I have a passion for programming and web development and I am not ashamed to admit I am a true nerd when it comes to my love of technology and learning.

From a young age, my love of working with computers blossomed into a passion for creating beautiful and inspiring websites that meet the needs of my users. I love problem solving and seeing my careful planning and design manifest into something I am truly proud of.

What I love about web development is that it is a perpetual learning experience that brings new dynamic challenges to the table each and every day. Whether it's working in a team environment and collaborating with colleagues, or tackling solo projects and going from analysis and design, to development and implementation -- I really love what I do and can't imagine doing anything else for a career!
